Cat Memorial Stones for Garden & Outdoor Tribute

A quiet engaging presence in the garden, a cat's memory deserves a touch of permanence.

Our collection of cat memorial stones is designed to reflect the elegance and spirit of your feline companion. Crafted from solid granite and natural stone, these markers provide a weatherproof tribute that blends naturally with your landscape.

We use deep engraving techniques to ensure that your cat's name and epitaph are permanently etched into the stone. From sleeping cat motifs to simple, elegant text, our cat grave markers are built to withstand the elements year-round.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are these stones safe for outdoor cat graves?
Absolutely. We use only outdoor-rated granite, river rock, and cast stone. They are impervious to moisture and frost, making them perfect for marking a backyard resting place.
Can I engrave a cat silhouette?
Yes. We have a wide selection of cat silhouettes, including sitting cats, sleeping cats, and playful poses. You can also upload your own custom design.
How heavy are the cat memorial stones?
Weights vary by size and material. Our river rocks typically weigh 5-15 lbs, while solid granite markers can weigh 15-25 lbs, ensuring they stay in place even in strong winds.
What is the turnaround time for a cat memorial?
Because every stone is custom engraved in our Arkansas studio, production typically takes 7-10 business days before shipping.
Do you make markers for other small pets?
Yes. While we specialize in dog and cat memorials, we frequently craft stones for rabbits, guinea pigs, and other beloved small companions.
