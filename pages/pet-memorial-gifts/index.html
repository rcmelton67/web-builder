Frequently Asked Questions

Is a memorial stone a good gift for pet loss?
Yes. Unlike flowers or cards, a memorial stone is a permanent tribute. It offers the grieving owner a physical place to visit and remember their pet, aiding in the healing process.
Can I have the gift shipped directly to the recipient?
Yes. We can ship directly to the grieving family. We do not include pricing in the packing slip for gift orders.
What if I don't know the dates?
It is common to gift a stone with just the pet's name or a simple phrase like 'Forever Loved'. You don't need dates to create a beautiful, meaningful tribute.
Do these gifts work for apartments?
Yes. Our smaller river rocks and cast stones are perfect for balconies, patios, or even indoor display on a bookshelf or mantle.
Can I add a gift message?
Yes. You can include a personalized gift note at checkout, which we will include with the package.
