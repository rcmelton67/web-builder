What Types of Pets Do You Make Memorial Stones For?

Love knows no species. We are honored to craft lasting tributes for every kind of companion animal that has touched your life.

Memorial Stones for Dogs

Dogs are often our most constant companions. For them, we craft markers that can withstand the elements of the backyard they loved to patrol. Popular engravings include paw prints, a favorite ball, or a simple "Loyal Friend."

Memorial Stones for Cats

For our feline friends, we often create slightly smaller, more delicate designs suitable for a quiet garden corner or a sunny spot on potio. Cat silhouettes and heart motifs are common choices.

Memorial Stones for Horses

Equine memorials require a presence that matches the animal's spirit. Our larger 12" x 8" granite stones are perfect for mounting on a barn wall, placing at a pasture gate, or marking a burial site. We can engrave horseshoes or breed-specific profiles.

Memorial Stones for Birds

From parrots to backyard songbirds, a small stone can mark a special spot under a feeder or tree. Our 3" x 2" stones are often perfect for small bird graves.

Reptiles and Small Pets

We have crafted stones for lizards, snakes, turtles, rabbits, guinea pigs, and hamsters. Every pet deserves to be remembered by name.

Custom Memorials for Any Companion

If you have a unique pet not listed here, rest assured we can likely create a beautiful tribute for them. Our custom pet memorial stones are blank canvases ready for your specific wording and tribute.

If you need help finding the right words, our guide on what to write on a pet memorial stone offers ideas for many different animals.

FAQ

Do you have specific clip art for different animals?
Yes, we have a library of silhouettes for dogs, cats, horses, birds, various reptiles, and small mammals.

Can I put a photo on the stone?
We specialize in deep engraving of text and graphics. For photo realism, we recommend laser-etched black granite, which we can discuss as a custom order.
