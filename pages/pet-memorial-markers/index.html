Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a good pet grave marker?
Durability is key. Granite and natural hard stone are the best choices because they do not absorb moisture, preventing cracking during freezing temperatures.
Can these markers be placed flat on the ground?
Yes. Our markers are heavy and stable enough to be placed directly on the earth, sod, or within a garden bed. They do not require a poured concrete foundation.
How deep is the engraving?
We deep-engrave all our stones, cutting well below the surface. We then treat the lettering with monument-grade lithichrome paint for maximum contrast and longevity.
Do you make custom shapes?
Our natural river rocks come in unique, organic shapes. For granite, we offer standard rectangular and square sizes that provide a traditional headstone appearance.
Can I install the marker myself?
Yes. No special tools are needed. simply clear a small area of ground to ensure it sits level, and place the stone. It's an easy DIY process.
