Frequently Asked Questions

How long will a dog memorial stone last outdoors?
Our granite and cast stone memorials are engineered for permanence. They are weatherproof, freeze-thaw resistant, and built to last safely outdoors for decades without fading or cracking.
Can I include my dog's paw print?
Yes. We can engrave custom paw prints. You can upload a photo of your dog's actual paw print, or choose from our library of breed-specific designs.
what is the best material for a dog garden marker?
Granite is the gold standard for durability and contrast. However, natural river rock offers a more organic, subtle look that blends beautifully into flower beds and gardens.
Do you offer different sizes for large dogs?
Yes. We offer stones ranging from small 6-inch markers up to large 12-inch+ monuments suitable for larger breeds or longer inscriptions.
How do I clean my dog's memorial stone?
Simple water and a soft brush are usually sufficient. For granite, you can use a mild dish soap. Avoid harsh chemicals or wire brushes that could damage the lithichrome paint.
